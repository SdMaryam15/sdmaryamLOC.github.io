Lochquarry Outdoor Centre

About our Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities. Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided.

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre provides outdoor activities for young people aged 6–18. The activities that the centre offers can be split into three main categories: land-based, water-based, and rope-based.
