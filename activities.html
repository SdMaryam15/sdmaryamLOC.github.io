Lochquarry Outdoor Centre

Activities

Land Based Activities

Visitors can take part in a wide range of land-based activities on and off site.

  • Hillwalking

    • From short walks around the site to Munro-bagging expeditions. Tailored to suit any age or experience, these adventures can last from one hour to all day. Max group size 12. Ages 6+.

  • Orienteering

    • Set in the centre’s grounds, find all the markers and show off your superior navigation skills. Groups of 2+, one map per group. Ages 6+.

  • Axe Throwing

    • Channel your inner Viking! Try hitting the target or throw yourself a bullseye. Max group size 6. Ages 10+.

Water Based Activities

Water-based activities take place on Lochquarry itself.

  • Kayaking

    • Paddle, roll, and raft in brand new kayaks. Max group size 8. Ages 8+.

  • Canoeing

    • Canoe the length of Lochquarry, and explore its islands with a picnic. Max group size 16. Ages 6+.

  • Powerboating

    • Take control of one of the Centre’s two RIBs and test your powerboating skills. Max group size 6. Ages 12+.

Rope Based Activities

All rope-based activities are on site, with full safety equipment provided.

  • Climbing

    • Scale the heights of the local quarry slabs. Max group size 8. Ages 8+.

  • Abseiling

    • Brave the descent and abseil down the quarry. Max group size 8. Ages 8+.

  • Pole Climb

    • Experience what it's like to be a telephone engineer and climb poles safely. Max group size 8. Ages 8+.
